How grading works. Strength uses published raw, drug-tested powerlifting deciles, then adjusts the weight-class thresholds by the study's age-band curves. To make the labels useful for a fit non-specialist, Average / Good / Great / Elite correspond to roughly the 10th / 30th / 50th / 70th percentiles of trained competitors. The mile uses age grading. Body composition uses same-sex/age waist percentiles, with height adding waist-to-height and BMI context. The three category scores receive equal weight.
